# About **Data-Blending Lab 2025**

**Data-Blending Lab 2025** is a two-day, invite-only datathon for our top annotators and data specialists.  
Instead of labeling new data, we’ll remix the gold mine we already own—combining complementary slices to unlock *fresh*, *high-value* training sets and quick-turn demo models.

---

## 🎯 Mission

1. **Prove the power of smart data composition**  
   - Beat an internal baseline *or* open a brand-new use-case.  
2. **Upskill annotators into data scientists**  
   - Hands-on with fine-tuning, evaluation and MLOps best practices.  
3. **Ship artifacts we can productionize**  
   - Reproducible notebooks, configs and concise documentation.

---

## 🤔 Why “Data Blending”?

> *“More” data ≠ “better” data*  
> But **better-chosen** data often beats bigger budgets.

Blending lets us:

| Benefit | Example |
|---------|---------|
| **Balance rare classes** | Add edge-case traffic scenes to a safety dataset. |
| **Create multi-modal pairs** | Align LiDAR frames with dash-cam video. |
| **Transfer label schemes** | Map coarse sentiment tags onto detailed emotion labels. |
| **Test generalization fast** | Train on Phone-Camera selfies → validate on Pro-DSLR images. |

---

## 👤 Who Should Participate?

- **Annotators** who know our datasets inside out and want to level-up their ML chops.  
- **Label-schema owners** who can spot synergies across projects.  
- **MLOps tinkerers** eager to automate pipelines and leaderboard dashboards.  
- **Storytellers** able to craft a clear “why this matters” narrative.

Teams of **3 – 5** blend these skills for maximum impact.

---

## 🛠️ What You’ll Build

1. **A blended dataset** (documented slice list + rationale).  
2. **A fine-tuned model** that beats or meets an agreed target metric.  
3. **A short pitch deck** (5 min) connecting the technical win to business value.  
4. **A replay script** so DI can reproduce results on fresh GPUs.
