## ℹ️ About the Datathon
**Why “data blending”?**  
Great models are built on great data—sometimes the fastest gains come not from collecting *more* data, but from **smart combinations** of what we already have: cross-modal pairs, complementary label schemas, or long-tail corner cases.

**What success looks like**
1. **Performance uplift** vs a standard baseline, *or* a convincing new business use-case.  
2. Fully reproducible pipeline: notebooks, configs, trained weights, and a short deck.  
3. Clear documentation so the DI team can take it forward.
